Ireland

Ireland's Education begins at 5 years of age and goes all the way to 18. From age 4/5-12 you are in primary school. The first year (4-5) you are in junior infants and then 5-6 you are in senior infants. Then you begin 1st class (7 years old) and go all the way to 6th class at 12 years of age. When you reach twelve you begin Secondary school and go to first year and then all the way up to 6th year (17-18). After that you reach College
