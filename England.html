How each England's school system works

    England
     The education system in england is divided into 4 parts:
      Primary: Primary generally starts at 5 and goes to 11
      Secondary: Secondary goes from 11-16 and will go to the GCSE's (The Irish equivalent being the Junior Cert)
      Further Education: Further Education for English Students will be 16-18 and will culminate in the A-Levels
      Higher/University: Higher education or university in England operates in a very similar way to most countries. Heres the latest updates from the Board of Education in the U.K.
